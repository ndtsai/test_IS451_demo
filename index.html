Factors Contributing to Nutritional Deficiencies

Nutritional deficiencies can arise due to various factors, including inadequate diet, poor absorption of nutrients, increased nutrient requirements during specific life stages, medical conditions, and food insecurity.

Effects of Specific Nutritional Deficiencies

Specific nutritional deficiencies can have significant effects on health:

  • Vitamin D deficiency: Weak bones, increased risk of fractures
  • Vitamin B12 deficiency: Anemia, neurological problems
  • Iron deficiency: Fatigue, weakness, pale skin
  • Iodine deficiency: Thyroid disorders, impaired cognitive function
  • Vitamin A deficiency: Night blindness, weakened immune system
